# Statistical physics: stochastic methods and optimization

This directory features computational models based on statistical mechanics, with a strong emphasis on stochastic algorithms and probability-based optimization methods.

## Key contents

* **Monte Carlo optimization (`monte_carlo_energy_optimization_constant_*.py`):** implementation of Monte Carlo algorithms to model complex probabilistic systems and optimize energy states. This scripts demonstrate a solid foundation in stochastic processes and randomized algorithms, methodologies that are highly transferable to machine learning, risk analysis, and operational research.

## Technical stack
* **Python 3**.
* **NumPy:** random number generation, statistical distributions and vectorized probability calculations.
* **SciPy:** advanced statistical functions and optimization routines.
* **Matplotlib:** statistical data visualization, histograms and convergence plotting.
